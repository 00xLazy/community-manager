# Community Challenge Templates

## Daily Challenges

### Knowledge Sharing
- "Share one useful tool or resource you discovered recently" — 10 pts
- "Explain a concept to a beginner in 3 sentences or less" — 15 pts
- "Post a code snippet that solved a problem for you today" — 10 pts

### Community Building
- "Welcome a new member and help them with their first question" — 15 pts
- "Give genuine feedback on someone's project in #showcase" — 10 pts
- "Translate a FAQ answer into another language" — 20 pts

### Fun
- "Share your desk setup in one photo" — 5 pts
- "What song are you coding to right now?" — 5 pts
- "Draw your current project architecture on a napkin" — 10 pts

## Weekly Quests

### Support Hero
- Goal: Answer 5 questions in #support
- Duration: 7 days
- Reward: 50 pts + Support Hero badge
- Difficulty: Medium

### Knowledge Curator
- Goal: Contribute 3 FAQ entries (via !save or reactions)
- Duration: 7 days
- Reward: 40 pts + Curator badge
- Difficulty: Medium

### Social Butterfly
- Goal: Start 2 discussions that each get 5+ replies
- Duration: 7 days
- Reward: 30 pts + Conversation Starter badge
- Difficulty: Easy

### Bug Hunter
- Goal: Report and document 3 bugs with reproduction steps
- Duration: 7 days
- Reward: 60 pts + Bug Hunter badge
- Difficulty: Hard

## Monthly Community Goals

### The 500 Club
- Goal: Community collectively answers 500 questions
- Everyone who participates gets Community Champion badge
- Top 3 contributors get bonus 100 pts

### Documentation Sprint
- Goal: Community creates 50 new knowledge base entries
- Tracked via knowledge extraction module
- Top contributor gets Documentation Hero badge

### Multilingual Month
- Goal: Translate 100 FAQ entries across 5+ languages
- Encourages diverse participation
- Special badge for each language contributed

## Point System

| Action | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Answer a question | 5 |
| Answer accepted (thanks/reaction) | 10 |
| Contribute FAQ entry | 15 |
| Start a popular discussion (5+ replies) | 10 |
| Complete daily challenge | varies |
| Complete weekly quest | varies |
| Help a newcomer | 15 |
| Report a valid bug | 20 |

# Win-Back Campaign Templates

## Timing

| Member Type | Wait Before Outreach | Cooldown Between Attempts |
|-------------|---------------------|--------------------------|
| Top contributor | 7 days inactive | 30 days |
| Regular active | 14 days inactive | 30 days |
| Occasional poster | 30 days inactive | 60 days |
| Newcomer (< 7 days) | 7 days inactive | Never repeat |

## Personalization Data Points

For each win-back message, gather:
1. Their top 3 expertise areas (from smart tags)
2. Unanswered questions matching their expertise
3. New content/features since they left
4. Their most interacted-with members (from social graph)
5. Community milestones they missed

## Message Templates

### Expert/Helper
Focus: "People need your help"
```
{unanswered_count} questions in your areas of expertise went unanswered.
Your {helpful_answers_count} past answers helped {unique_helped_count} people.
```

### Social Member
Focus: "Here's what you missed"
```
Hot topics: {top_3_discussions}
Community milestone: {recent_milestone}
Your friends have been active: {familiar_names}
```

### Creator
Focus: "Your content is still valued"
```
Your {content_title} was referenced {reference_count} times since you left.
{new_related_content} new content in your areas of interest.
```

### Newcomer
Focus: "We want to help you get started"
```
Resources to get started: {starter_links}
Avg response time: {avg_response_min} minutes
Mentor available: {mentor_suggestion}
```

## A/B Testing

Track which message styles work best:
- Personalized vs generic
- Short vs detailed
- Question-focused vs social-focused
- With community stats vs without

Update templates quarterly based on response rates.

## Do NOT Contact

- Members who explicitly left (posted goodbye)
- Members who were banned or warned
- Members who unsubscribed from DMs
- Members inactive for 90+ days (likely abandoned account)
